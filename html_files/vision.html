ABOUT

My Blood bank is a subset of Appolllo Hospital. We at the bank test, collect and separate the blood according to the categories and components. Each component of the blood is stored properly labeled and tracked for future use. We take utmost care of the donor and the blood with our dedicated staff and technology. After collection, it is then released for distribution to whoever requests and needs the blood, thereby changing lives.

OUR VISION

God gives us life, blood banks saves our lives. Our motto “Give Blood, Give Life, Bring Smile” means we bring cheers to our customers. Customers who frantically come to us to save their beloved ones find it cheering after receiving blood and knowing that blood was available to them throughout the whole ordeal. Three cheers to our blood bank for bringing smiles.

Our Vision serves as a framework for our organization to accomplish our goals.

  1. Act with urgency – Responding to emergency situations
  2. Do efficiently - By developing skills
  3. Be generous and compassionate – To patients in their troubled times
  4. Spreading the importance of donating – Donate blood to save lives
